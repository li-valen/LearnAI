What actually is AI?

AI is trying to mimic the way humans think and act, but with a computer. The AI is a series of neural networks (which are like the neurons in our brains) and complex statistical computations that allow it to understand what we say and see.

Everything AI does is based on numbers. It converts images, videos, audios into numbers. And this allows the AI to see things the way us humans see things. AI models go through thousands of trial and error rounds before being able to predict on new images and videos.

Benefits

The AI that you and I use such as Siri and google assistant and a lot of other AI’s like ChatGPT or Googles Bart are all large models. These models are millions of connections that build the models memory and its ability to think of new things.

These models are like the neurons in our brains, they allow the model to build strong neural connections. These models are trained in a series of trial and error simulations called epochs.

Each epoch represent are number of trial and error simulations where the model is trying to learn about the dat you are feeding to it. If its images, it breaks down the images to numbers and then learns and predicts what it is.

Drawbacks

AI’s are everywhere, and as many of you know AI is creeping more and more into our lives. What type of AI’s are we leaving with now.

As you already know, Siri, Alexa, and Google assistant are AI’s that constantly listen and use your voice to learn and understand how humans ask questions and what to respond.

AI’s in photo processing are also used, when you take a photo, the AI in the phone is what makes the picture look good. It understand the foreground and background just like a human.
